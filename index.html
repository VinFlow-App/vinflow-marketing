AI Engine

Specialized agents, not a generic chatbot.

Each AI agent is purpose-built for a specific role — trained on real dealership data, your strategy profiles, and market conditions. They work alongside your team, not as a novelty.

🎯 Buyer Agent
📊 Inventory Agent
🔍 Appraisal Agent

VinFlow's AI isn't a single model answering questions. It's a system of specialized agents that understand your inventory strategy, profit targets, market position, and buying history. Each agent operates within guardrails you configure — no black box decisions.

Buyer Agent

Scans auction run lists and scores every vehicle against your strategy profile. Identifies units that match your inventory gaps and profit targets before the lane opens.

"14 vehicles match your acquisition criteria in tomorrow's ACV run. 3 are high-confidence buys under your max bid threshold."
Inventory Agent

Monitors aging, market shifts, and competitive pricing daily. Flags vehicles that need repricing or remarketing before carrying costs erode margin.

"6 units crossed 45 days today. 2 have dropped below cost-to-market. Repricing recommendations ready for review."
Appraisal Agent

Aggregates market data, history reports, and reconditioning estimates into a single buy/pass recommendation with confidence scoring on every data point.

"2019 RAM 1500 Laramie — Flow Score 7.2. Clean title, 1-owner. Market position: $2,400 below average. Recommend buy at $28,500."
